Redesign of customer details. Adds an Overview card at the top of the customer detail page showing Total orders (with the most recent order's date), Last login, and per-currency Spent in recent orders + Avg order value cards.

The Spent / Avg order value cards are aggregated over the customer's last 10 orders, broken down by currency — so a customer with orders in USD and EUR sees one card pair per currency rather than having the metric hidden.
